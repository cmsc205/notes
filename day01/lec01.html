Advice

  • You are going to get frustrated, you're learning a new language
  • Look at your data
  • Don't thrash
  • Do the most braindead thing first, take it end to end, then iterate/improve.
  • "You actually dont know what you are doing until after you have done it."
  • Think of the marginal return of your efforts.
